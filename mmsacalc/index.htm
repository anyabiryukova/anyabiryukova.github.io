This calculator works by adding the times required to make the food and dividing them by the customer offer. If the customer wants a food that requires other foods as ingredients, it adds in the times required to make the foods that are the ingredients for the food the customer wants.
The calculator does not take into account the times for harvesting crops, honey, milk, pork, eggs, or trees.
